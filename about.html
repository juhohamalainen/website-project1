About Us



Why we started Watcher

Watcher started with a young team of University students. Only too well did we know the feeling of getting eyeballed at the concessionaire, experiencing a retail experience we perceived as outdated. Red carpets, bouncers, all the bling-bling.


How Watcher works

We have established a network of several hundred European authorized dealers and Swiss manufacturers. This flexibility to buy from multiple suppliers allows us to always offer you the most competitive prices. After placing your order, we source your watch from within our network and deliver it with much love to your wrist.


Why choose Watcher?

There are many reasons why you should buy your watch from Watcher. Watcher bears a legacy of quality, service, and flawless delivery. Buy yours today!
